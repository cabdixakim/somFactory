MANUFACTURING PROCESS

Our products are manufactured by using the modern technology in the field of plastic extruders. The manufacturing processes are specially taken care with in house quality control to ensure that the end product meets quality, reliability to the climatic condition of the EAST AFRIC  region. Besides in house quality control, tests are also carried out through independent laboratories of international standards to Certifythe quality of our products.
